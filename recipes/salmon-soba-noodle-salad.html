Salmon Soba Noodle Salad Recipe

Total Time

Prep Time

Cook Time

25 min

15 min

10 min

Description

A healthy salad featuring moist medium-cooked salmon fillets drizzled with ginger dressing over soba noodles and veggies.

Instructions

  1. Remove salmon fillets from the fridge to bring them up closer to room temperature.
  2. Cut spinach (or baby bok choy, yu choy sum) into 2 1/2-inch long sections.
    1. Blanch in boiling water quickly until vegetables are cooked to your liking.
    2. Transfer vegetables to a bowl of cold water to stop the cooking. Then drain and gently squeeze out some excess water. Set aside
  3. Cook dried soba noodles according to package instructions. Then rinse under cold water thoroughly to remove sliminess and stickiness. Drain and set aside.
  4. Mix together all ingredients to make the dressing. Taste and adjust to your liking. Add some black pepper to taste. Set aside
  5. Place a pan over medium to medium-high heat. Once the pan is hot, add some oil to coat the bottom. Pat dry the salmon fillets and place them skin side down on the pan.
  6. Cook for 3-4 minutes to let the skin turn golden and crisp up.
    1. Then season the top with some salt and pepper, flip over and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
    2. Adjust heat as needed and aim for medium-cooked salmon.
  7. Place soba noodles, vegetables and salmon in serving bowls/plates. Serve immediately with the ginger dressing.

Ingredients

  • 2 Salmon Fillets (about 6-8 oz each and up to 1 1/2-inch thick)
  • 6-8 oz Spinach (or baby bok choy, yu choy sum)
  • 6 oz Dry Soba Noodles
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cooking Oil

For the Dressing

  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 2 tsp Honey
  • 2 tsp Hot Water
  • 3/4 tsp Ginger, grated
