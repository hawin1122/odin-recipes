Tofu Soba Noodle Stir Fry Recipe

Total Time

Prep Time

Cook Time

30 min

20 min

10 min

Description

Soba noodles featuring a delicious sesame sauce with marinated tofu and stir-fried veggies and fresh herbs for a quick and easy meal bowl perfect for lunch, dinner or meal prep!

Instructions

  1. Drain the tofu and lay it on its side for a few minutes to let the water drain
  2. In a medium size shallow dish, whisk together the tamari, rice wine vinegar, maple syrup, sesame oil, garlic, ginger, red pepper flakes.
  3. Cut the tofu into 1/2 inch cubes. Add the tofu to the marinade and let set for 20 minutes, turning at least once halfway through. The tofu is meant to be served at room temp straight from the marinade. Feel free to warm it up by adding it to the pan while stir frying the veggies.
  4. While tofu is marinating, make the soba noodles according to package directions. Rinse well under cool running water to cool the noodles and stop them continuing to cook.
  5. Heat sesame oil over medium heat, add the broccoli and carrots, stir fry for 3 minutes.
    1. Add a large spoon of marinade, basil and mint, cook another 1 â€“ 2 minutes.
    2. Also, you can add the tofu, marinade and noodles to the pan, tossing well, to warm through.
  6. Serve soba noodle in individual dishes topped with veggies and tofu, and top with remaining marinade, scallions, sesame seeds and additional fresh herbs.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz Soba Noodles
  • 10 oz Tofu (firm or extra firm), cubed
  • 1 handful Broccoli Florets
  • 2 Carrots, sliced diagonally
  • 2 Scallions, thinly sliced
  • 1 small handful Mint Leaves
  • 1 small handful Basil Leaves
  • 1 tbsp Toasted Sesame Oil, for sauteing
  • (Optional) Sesame Seeds, for garnish

For the Marinade

  • 1/4 cup Tamari (or Soy Sauce)
  • 2 tbsp Rice Wine Vinegar
  • 2 tbsp Pure Maple Syrup
  • 1 tbsp Sesame Oil
  • 2 Garlic Cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp Red Pepper Flakes, or to taste
